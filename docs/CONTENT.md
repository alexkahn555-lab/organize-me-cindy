# CONTENT.md — page content map

This is the structure, media assignment, and copy direction per page. It is not
finished copy. Finished copy is written in Phase 4 against this map. Copy voice:
warm, plain, emotionally specific, complete sentences, no buzzwords, no
em-dashes. Sell relief and calm first, list rooms second.

## Home

1. Hero. Emotional outcome plus geography plus one trust cue plus one CTA.
   Direction: "Calm, organized homes across DC and Northern Virginia." Subline
   names bonded and insured. Primary CTA: "Request a free consultation."
   Background: one of the 30 full-res "after" images.
2. Trust strip. Bonded and insured. Serves DC, Alexandria, Arlington, Fairfax,
   McLean, Vienna, Falls Church. Google review source.
3. Proof cluster. Three to four before/after pairs from the gallery, strongest
   first (closet and kitchen read best). CTA repeats here.
4. Services menu. Cards by room and life event, each linking to its service page.
5. Process. Four steps: consultation, plan, hands-on organizing, maintenance.
6. Testimonials. Two or three specific quotes.
7. FAQ teaser. Three high-anxiety questions: do I have to be present, what gets
   discarded, is my home kept private. Link to full FAQ.
8. Footer CTA plus trust cues repeated.

## Before & After (centerpiece)

Categorized gallery grouped by room type. Each entry is a labeled side-by-side
or stacked pair with a caption: room, place, challenge, intervention, outcome.
Sliders only as optional lightbox after click, never as the default view.

Video before/after pairs to feature prominently:
- Bedroom: First Look at Kelly's Bedroom -> Finished Bedroom.
- Bathroom: First Look at Kelly's Bathroom -> Finished Bathroom.
- Closet seasonal: Spring/Summer Closet and Fall/Winter Closet.

Caption example: "Arlington pantry. Mixed storage and expired items. Categorized
by meal type, donated duplicates, added clear labels. Restocking now takes one
pass."

(multi-select), timeline, optional message, optional photo upload. Posts to
Resend. Short, above the fold, no burying.

Show phone 571-212-9299 and service area.

garage, kitchen). They carry videos and have SEO value. Lower priority than the
core pages. Keep original URLs or 301 them to preserve any ranking.
